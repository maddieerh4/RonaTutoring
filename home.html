Why Rona Tutoring?

Our teachers are driven, persistent, motivated, and dedicated to making sure that our students learn. We hope by providing these free resources, students can stay motivated and on top of their school material and stay even when they're stuck at home.

Our Mission

Rona Tutoring was founded in March by three high schoolers trying to make a difference. Our mission is to bridge the learning gap caused by community disruptions by providing tutors to those whose learning has been affected. We cover any subject from Pre-K to 12th grade but we primarily tutor in STEM subjects and English. All for free!

Qualifications

All of our tutors are highly qualified scholars with a deep understanding and knowledge of their respective topics, having taken advanced classes such as linear algebra, multivariable calculus, and physics with calculus.
