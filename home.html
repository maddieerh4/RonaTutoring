Our Mission

Rona Tutoring was founded in March by three high schoolers trying
to make a difference. Our mission is to bridge the learning gap caused
by community disruptions by providing tutors to those whose learning
has been affected. We cover any subject from Pre-K to 12th grade but we
primarily tutor in STEM subjects and English. All for free!

Our Hope

The COVID-19 pandemic has altered education worldwide, and in
most cases, resulted in the loss of learning opportunities and
less engaged learning. We hope by providing these free resources,
students can stay on top of their school material and stay motivated
to learn, even when they're stuck at home.
